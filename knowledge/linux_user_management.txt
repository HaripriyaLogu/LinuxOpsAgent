Linux User Management
============================================================

1. User Management Overview
1. Create User: useradd -m username
1. Set Password: passwd username
1. Delete User: userdel -r username
1. Modify User: usermod
1. Groups: groupadd, groupdel, usermod -aG
1. Lock/Unlock: passwd -l / passwd -u
1. Sudo configuration: visudo
1. Important files: /etc/passwd, /etc/shadow, /etc/group
1. Troubleshooting: permission denied, locked account

Best Practice:
- Validate before production.
- Monitor logs.
- Follow least privilege.
